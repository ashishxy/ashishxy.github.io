how to learn stuff in a time where attention spans are horrible?

1.avoid distractions. use the smartphone as if it is your servant, do not let it become your master.

2. too much information is not always helpful. you do not necessarily need 5 tutorials, instead you need 5 ways to think about it. sit with the problem.

3. take the information you just consumed and write it down in your own words. figure out what you do not understand yet.

4. try to share it with your friends. try to explain it to them. explaining will reveal holes in your understanding which you never knew existed.
